Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Platform

Executive Summary

Global supply chains face constant shocks — from geopolitics to extreme weather. Current monitoring tools react only after delays and cost spikes occur. This platform transforms unstructured news into structured Risk Indices and Alerts, helping leaders anticipate disruptions, diversify suppliers, and reroute logistics proactively.

Solution

  • Multi-source news ingestion (MVP: NewsAPI).
  • Risk scoring engine (NLP + GPT + keyword/entity patterns).
  • Aggregation into risk indices by corridor, port, and supplier.
  • Executive summaries in plain language.
  • Dashboards with risk graphs, heatmaps, and alerts.
  • Exports: CSV, PDF, or Power BI integration.

Key Outputs

  • Risk Table: Daily corridor & port scores by disruption type.
  • Trendlines: 30-day sentiment and risk intensity visualized.
  • Daily Brief: Auto-generated summaries and top flagged articles.
  • Exports: CSV / PDF / Email digest.

Spotify vs Local Music Library Comparator

Overview

As a DJ and music enthusiast, I often manage playlists on Spotify while also keeping a large collection of downloaded tracks on my desktop. But sometimes I’d forget to download songs or accidentally skip a few, leading to gaps between my Spotify playlists and my local library.

To solve this, I built a Python tool that automatically compares a Spotify playlist against my local music folder, identifies which songs are missing, and even creates a new Spotify playlist called “Missing Songs” with the unmatched tracks.

Problem

DJing requires a complete local library (you can use online platforms but most locations have unreliable wifi) — but manually cross-checking 100s of tracks between Spotify and my computer was inefficient. File naming differences (e.g., Artist - Title vs. different metadata tags) made it harder to track missing songs by eye.

  • Compare Spotify playlists with my desktop files.
  • Identify missing songs reliably.
  • Generate a “catch-up” playlist so I could download and prepare them quickly.

Solution

  • Fetch Spotify Playlist Tracks: OAuth + extract playlist tracks.
  • Scan Local Music Directory: Extract metadata or filenames from local audio files.
  • Compare Track Lists: Use RapidFuzz fuzzy matching to flag missing tracks.
  • Generate “Missing Songs” Playlist: Auto-create a Spotify playlist with unmatched tracks.

Tech Stack

  • Python, Spotipy, Mutagen, RapidFuzz, dotenv, logging
  • Spotify Web API (OAuth authentication, playlist creation)
  • Local music scanning with ID3 metadata parsing

Impact

  • Automated playlist reconciliation — saves hours of manual checking.
  • Prevents DJ set issues by ensuring no missing tracks.
  • Scalable — works for any Spotify playlist and any local music directory.
  • Extendable — can evolve into a Streamlit web app for GUI-based usage.
