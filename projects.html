Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Platform

Overview

Global supply chains face shocks from geopolitics, labor, logistics, and climate. Existing tools react too late. This platform converts real-time news into structured Risk Indices and Alerts to help leaders anticipate disruptions and respond proactively.

Solution

  • Multi-source news ingestion (MVP: NewsAPI)
  • NLP + GPT-powered risk scoring
  • Aggregated corridor/port risk indices
  • Plain-language executive summaries
  • Dashboards with heatmaps & alerts
  • Exports: CSV, PDF, Power BI

Outputs

  • Risk tables, trendlines, and daily briefs
  • Automated alerts for emerging threats

Commodity Stockout Risk Platform

Overview

A Streamlit-powered platform that uses financial market signals as a proxy for supply chain stockout risk. By analyzing commodity prices, trading volume, and technical indicators, it generates a Stockout Risk Index and predicts potential disruptions.

Features

  • Live OHLCV commodity data from Yahoo Finance
  • Indicators: ATR, RSI, MACD, volatility & volume
  • Risk Index: unified 0–1 score
  • ML: Random Forest shock-event predictor
  • GPT-powered natural language summaries

Spotify vs Local Music Library Comparator

Overview

DJs often juggle Spotify playlists and local track libraries, risking gaps before gigs. This tool compares a Spotify playlist with local files, flags missing tracks, and even generates a new Spotify playlist called “Missing Songs”.

Solution

  • Fetch Spotify playlists via OAuth
  • Scan local directories for metadata
  • Fuzzy matching with RapidFuzz
  • Auto-generate a Spotify “catch-up” playlist

Impact

  • Saves hours of manual checking
  • Ensures DJs have complete libraries offline
  • Scalable & extendable into a GUI/Streamlit app
